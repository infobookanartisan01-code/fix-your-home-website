How the App Works

For Customers

  1. Open the app and go to Request Artisan.
  2. Select the artisan category you need (Electrician, Plumber, etc.).
  3. Fill in your details and your job details (title, description, and address).
  4. (Optional) Add a job photo to help artisans understand the work.
  5. Tap Submit request. Your request is sent to artisans in that category.
  6. Artisans can Accept your request. You may receive multiple accepts.
  7. Choose one artisan. After choosing, you can call or WhatsApp them directly.
  8. After the job, you can rate the artisan to help other customers.

For Artisans

  1. Register your business and upload your profile photos (profile, qualifications, previous work).
  2. Wait for admin approval (activation) to go live on the app.
  3. Log in using Artisan Login.
  4. You will see job requests that match your trade categories.
  5. Accept or decline each job request.
  6. If the customer chooses you, you can contact the customer and complete the work.
  7. Keep your profile updated so customers can see your best photos and information.

Admin / Control

  • The admin can activate or deactivate artisans.
  • The admin can manage ratings (edit or delete if needed).
  • The monthly / registration fee per trade can be updated by the admin.
